Dal Giardino Risotto Rice with Mushrooms 411g

Pack number 123 is a very special pack of Dal Giardino Risotto Rice with Mushrooms that was hand crafted by Mathilda's team on the morning of Tuesday 5th May 2020. You can see their signatures on the back of the pack. Mathilda remarked "I remember that morning as we had to wait until gone 10 o'clock for the fresh mushrooms to arrive."
